Motivation



According to , the keyword 'Cherry-sumers' is included in the 2023 trend keywords. 'Cherry-sumers' refers to strategically minded consumers who have emerged due to the proliferation of single-person households and the global economic crisis, aiming to efficiently utilize limited resources. As evident from the emergence of this keyword, the demand for personalized services targeting a new consumer base has become prominent.

Effort

Increase in Enrollment Rate: Through personalized services targeting specific groups, companies will provide more tailored cards to increase the enrollment rate. Expansion of Card Usage: Through segmented benefits and recommendations, customers will use their cards more frequently, enabling companies to increase the overall card usage rate. Understanding Consumer Spending Patterns: With a clear understanding of their spending patterns, individuals can efficiently utilize resources. Acquiring Customized Benefits: By using a card tailored to their needs, individuals can maximize benefits, gaining access to a wider range of advantages.

How to use homepage

Main page :
a page for quickly checking each consumer persona - CardDNA


1. The page categorizes groups into 16 distinct CardDNA profiles.
2. Clicking on each option allows users to explore the characteristics of the corresponding CardDNA.

Get personalized card recommendations :
a page to view CardDNA based on personal spending history


1. Users enter the card number they primarily use.
2. The service analyzes the spending history associated with the card number and reveals the consumer persona, CardDNA.
3. It recommends cards with benefits tailored to the identified persona group.
4. If users decide to issue a card through this service, it assists them in customizing the card cover.
